Instructions

  1. To initiate a game, press the "Start Game" button located at the bottom of the screen.
  2. A sequence of cards will be revealed for a brief period.
  3. You need to memorize those cards and choose them when they close.
  4. You must select them in the correct order, starting from one.
  5. When you successfully select all the correct cards, you will level up. You will receive an additional try and advance one level.
  6. To progress to the next level, you need to wait for the timer displayed on the board to run out, or you can press the "Continue" button on the board.
  7. You can pause before the new level starts by either refreshing the page to stop the timer or by closing the tab with the game.
  8. The maximum level you can reach is level 20, which is the most challenging. As the levels increase, the number of cards you need to remember also increases.
  9. The maximum level you've reached will be saved.
